The Kingdom Of Games

This story goes back to the 2000s in my home country, the Czech Republic. Many families had a popular board game collection called Kingdom of Games.
This site is meant to recreate two of my favorite games from that collection, which I used to play with my grandparents.

Main Menu Worms Geese

Game 1: Žížaly (Worms)

Worms (an adaptation of snakes and ladder) are played with one 6 sided die. The winner is who reach the goal first. You may encounter ladders, which makes your way to the goal faster. Be careful though, there are evil worms on the way, which will set you back!!

Game 2: Husičky (Geese)

Geese are played with one 6 sided die. Help the goose to reach the goal first. However, the path is not straightforward. Some tiles can help you to achieve to goal faster. Some may slow you down!!
